# **App Name**: Aceh Flood Insight

## Core Features:

- Article Aggregation: Aggregate news articles and social media posts related to the Aceh flood from various online sources.
- Sentiment Analysis: Perform sentiment analysis on the aggregated data to gauge public perception and emotional response.
- Timeline Visualization: Create a timeline to visualize the sequence of events, media coverage, and government responses.
- Government Response Tracking: Monitor and track government announcements, aid distribution, and policy changes related to the flood.
- Data Filtering: Filter news based on media types, sources, regions, and government titles to deliver tailored information.
- Reporting Tool: Generates summary reports about communication of mass media.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Deep blue (#3F51B5) to reflect the somber mood and seriousness of the analysis.
- Background color: Light gray (#F5F5F5) to provide a clean, neutral backdrop.
- Accent color: Soft orange (#FFAB40) to highlight key information and call-to-action elements.
- Body and headline font: 'Inter' for a clean and modern look, suitable for both headlines and body text.
- Use clear, representative icons to illustrate data types and response metrics.
- Implement a clean, intuitive layout to enhance readability and data comprehension.
- Subtle animations to transition between different datasets.
